Peach Cobbler

How to make the best peach cobbler ever!

This recipe is made from scratch and made with fresh ingredients and love

Ingredients

Peach Cobbler Filling

  • Fresh Peaches
  • Lemon Juice
  • Brown Sugar
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Cornstarch

Cobbler Topping

  • All-purpose Flour
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Salted Butter
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Peel and slice peaches
  2. Making the Filling

    In a small bowl, mix together brown sugar, granulated sugar, cinnamon, cornstarch and sea salt then set aside

    In a large bowl, combine peaches, lemon juice and vanilla. Then add dry ingredients and stir to combine. Set the peach cobbler filling aside

  3. Making the Cobbler Topping

    Start by mixing the dry ingredients together, then cutting in the butter with your hands(or fork or pastry cutter) until butter is evenly distributed but still "clumpy".

    Add the milk and vanilla and stir to combine.The biscuit batter should be slightly tacky but not unmanageable or too liquid

  4. Putting it together

    Transfer the peach filling to a 9 x 13 baking dish.

    Spread the batter for the topping over the peach filling

    Then sprinkle the biscuit topping with the turbiando sugar

  5. Bake & Cool

    Bake until the biscuits are just barely browned and set and the filling is bubbling

    Let it cool for at least 10 minutes before serving it warm.

  6. Serve

    Serve warm with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream and an extra sprinkle of cinnamon sugar.

  7. Store

    Store any leftovers in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 5 days or in the freezer for up to 1 month.
